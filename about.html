About CookieShop

Your No.1 Bakery

Welcome to our bakery, pallies! We specialize in freshly baked breads, pastries, cakes, and cookies made with only the finest ingredients. Our breads are made from a blend of carefully selected flours and fermented for optimal flavor and texture. We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, and our pastries are made with real butter and free-range eggs. We have a wide variety of breads available, including sourdough, whole wheat, and rye. Our pastry selection includes classic favorites like croissants, Danish, and muffins, as well as seasonal specialties like pumpkin pie and gingerbread cookies. Though we are most famous for our cookies, hence the name. Try them. They are GOOD - and not going to wreck your teeth. We also offer a variety of cakes for all occasions, from birthdays to weddings. In addition to our delicious baked goods, we also offer catering services for events of all sizes. From corporate gatherings to weddings, we can customize a menu to fit your needs. Don't forget to follow us on social media to see our daily specials and stay updated on our newest creations!
