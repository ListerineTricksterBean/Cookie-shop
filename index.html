Welcome To CookieShop

Indulge In A Fresh Bakery Experience

Ah, CookieShop. Filled with cookies, sweets, choco bars, a lively, memorable experience bursting into Bradford. Welcome, friend! We specialize in homemade, delicious cookies that are baked fresh daily. Our selection includes classic favorites like chocolate chip and oatmeal raisin, as well as unique flavors like salted caramel and macadamia nut. All of our cookies are made with high-quality, natural ingredients. They are also free of preservatives. Our goal is lift a burden off your mind with rich flavours, not ground and pound your teeth with dross as is the standard nowadays. We'll change that. We take pride in using only the best butter, sugar, flour, and other ingredients to ensure a perfect taste and texture in every bite.

In addition to our regular flavors, we also offer seasonal and limited-edition cookies, so be sure to check back often for new and exciting options. We also offer gift boxes and custom orders for special occasions such as weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. So, stop by our store or visit us online to place an order. Come let us satisfy that sweet tooth!

Susan Powell Owner

Freshly Baked

Food Crafted With The Very Best Passion, The Very Best Love And Organic Ingredients

The folk at CookieShop are wholesome, honest, and kind. Our food overflows with these same qualities. We take pride in sourcing our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers, ensuring the freshest and highest quality products for our customers. Our breads are made with flour from a family-owned mill just a few miles away, and our fruits and berries come from nearby orchards and farms.

Our dairy products are sourced from a local creamery. Our eggs? A nearby farm that raises chickens humanely. Our chocolate, found in the most popular products, is produced in the Caribbean, blossoming from within a cocoa pod on a cocoa tree beneath the high, blazing sun. The sugar content is stripped down to the bare minimum. Because try as you might, you just can't get rid of it without sacrificing taste; so we stuck to our golden recipe, ensuring a taste sensation yet also something good on the gut. Pick us. These are unbelievably healthy, yet bargain-priced, ingredients. We believe in supporting our community and the hardworking individuals who provide us with the ingredients for our delicious baked goods. From our buttery croissants to our homemade jams, every bite is a taste of the local community. We invite you to come and experience the difference that fresh, locally sourced ingredients make in our baked goods. Our Selection
